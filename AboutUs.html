Who We Are

At Slovenské Vzdelávacie Centrum – South Manchester, we are a registered charity dedicated to preserving and promoting Slovak language, culture, and traditions. We offer Slovak language education for both children and adults and organize cultural events that bring our community together.

While we primarily support Slovak-speaking families, everyone who loves Slovak culture is welcome to join our activities!

Our Mission

  • Keep Slovak heritage alive for future generations
  • Support children growing up abroad to stay connected to their roots
  • Create a vibrant and welcoming Slovak community in the UK

We believe that language is more than just words – it is identity, history, and belonging.

Our Journey

Founded by a passionate team of Slovak teachers and volunteers, Slovenské Vzdelávacie Centrum officially became a registered charity on 9th September 2024.

Our grand opening, celebrated with the Faculty of Education from Univerzita Mateja Bela and the Association of Slovak Schools and Community Centres in the UK, marked the beginning of an exciting journey to unite and support Slovak families living abroad.

Today, we continue to grow by offering high-quality Slovak education, cultural workshops, and strong community support.

What We Offer

  • Saturday Slovak Language Classes (for all ages)
  • Cultural Events and Workshops (like Easter crafts, Christmas carols, and traditional celebrations)
  • Community Support (help for families moving between Slovakia and the UK)
  • Fun Social Activities (where friendships are made!)
